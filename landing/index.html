The Problem

😰
"My child is late from school and won't pick up the phone"

Every minute feels like an hour. No update, no location — just anxiety building up.

🚨
"How do I alert my family in an emergency?"

An accident, getting lost, feeling unsafe — and you feel helpless because there's no system in place.

Gravity solves all three problems in one app — battery-friendly, real-time, and built for the whole family.
