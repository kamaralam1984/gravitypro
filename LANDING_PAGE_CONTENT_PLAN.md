## JO MISSING HAI — Priority Wise

---

### 🔴 HIGH PRIORITY (Conversion ke liye zaroori)

---

#### SECTION A — Problem / Pain Point (Hero ke baad)
**Position:** Hero aur Features ke beech
**Title:** *"Kya aapko pata hai abhi aapka bachcha kahan hai?"*
**Content:**
- 3 emotional pain points (cards ya icons ke saath):
  - 😰 "Bachcha school se ghar late ho gaya — koi update nahi"
  - 📵 "Phone busy, message no reply — anxiety badh rahi hai"
  - 🚨 "Emergency mein family tak khabar kaise pahunche?"
- Ek line solution: **"Gravity in sab ka jawab hai — real time, 24/7."**

**Kyun zaroori:** Indian/Kenyan/UAE market mein emotionally driven purchase hota hai. Pain point pehle dikhao, phir solution.

---

#### SECTION B — App Screenshots / Phone Mockups
**Position:** Features ke baad
**Title:** *"Dekhiye Gravity andar se"*
**Content:**
- 4-5 real app screenshots phone frame mein:
  1. Live map with family members
  2. SOS alert screen
  3. Geofence zone setup
  4. Battery + status dashboard
  5. Alert notification
- Horizontal scroll ya slider (mobile-friendly)

**Kyun zaroori:** User app download karne se pehle UI dekhna chahta hai. Bina screenshots ke bounce rate 40%+ badh jaata hai.

---

**Position:** Features ke baad, Download se pehle

---

#### SECTION D — SOS Feature Spotlight
**Position:** Features section ke baad (dedicated)
**Title:** *"Emergency mein 1 button — poori family ko alert"*
**Content:**
- Full-width dark red/green gradient section
- SOS button ka visual (animated pulse)
- 3 points:
  - 📍 Live location automatically share hoti hai
  - 🔔 Poore family circle ko instant notification
  - 📞 Emergency contacts ko auto-call option
- Testimonial: *"Meri beti ne SOS press kiya — 4 minute mein main wahan tha." — Rajesh, Mumbai*

**Kyun zaroori:** SOS sabse powerful differentiator hai Gravity ka. Iska dedicated section conversion rate 25%+ badhata hai.

---

#### SECTION E — For Parents vs For Children (Two Column)
**Position:** How It Works ke baad
**Title:** *"Sabke liye alag experience"*
**Content:**
**Parent Side (Green):**
- Poore family ka live map
- Geofence alerts
- SOS notifications
- Battery levels dekhna
- Location history
- Circle manage karna

**Child Side (Blue):**
- Simple 1-button SOS
- Family ko apni location share karna
- "I'm Safe" quick message
- Parents ka location dekhna
- Privacy controls

- CTA buttons: "Parent Panel Try Karo →" | "Child Panel Try Karo →"

**Kyun zaroori:** Different users ke liye different value proposition clearly dikhani chahiye.

## DESIGN NOTES

- **Font:** Plus Jakarta Sans (already used) — rakho
- **Color:** Dark green `#050C08` bg, `#00E676` accent — premium lagta hai, rakho
- **Mobile first:** 60%+ traffic mobile se aayega
- **CTA buttons:** Har 3-4 sections ke baad ek CTA zaroor ho
- **Animations:** Scroll reveal already hai — aur micro-animations features cards pe add karo
- **Images:** Diverse families dikhao — Indian, Kenyan, UAE — stock photos nahi, illustrated mockups better hain
- **Load speed:** Images WebP format mein rakho, lazy load karo
